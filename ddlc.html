Introduction

On this page, i'll write how I manage to create a mod for DDLC, a visual novel game.

Step 1: what's a visual novel game?

According to Wikipedia, "A visual novel (VN) is a form of digital interactive fiction. Visual novels are often associated with the medium of video games, but are not always labeled as such themselves. They combine a textual narrative with static or animated illustrations and a varying degree of interactivity. The format is more rarely referred to as novel game, a retranscription of the wasei-eigo term noberu gēmu (ノベルゲーム), which is more often used in Japanese."
